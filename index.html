      Why an OpenRouteService key?

      OpenRouteService often produces the most polished cycling routes, but it requires a free API key. Valhalla works without one — you can switch any time.

      1. Open the OpenRouteService signup page.
      2. Create a free account and verify your email.
      3. Create a new token in the dashboard.
      4. Paste the key here and click Save.

      Your key stays in your browser — it's never sent anywhere except directly to OpenRouteService.
