      Elevation profile

      Map tiles: CyclOSM (OpenStreetMap). Elevation: OpenTopoData public API. Routing: OpenRouteService (API key) or Valhalla (no key). This page is static and runs entirely in your browser.

      Why an OpenRouteService key?

      OpenRouteService provides the best cycling and running routing, but it requires a free API key. Your key stays in your browser only.

      1. Open the OpenRouteService signup page.
      2. Create a free account and verify your email.
      3. Create a new token in the dashboard.
      4. Paste the key here and click “Save”.
